Our Story

Rooted in the Island, Raised on Good Coffee

North Shore Roasters began as a single roaster in a garage, fueled by a deep love for the craft and the land. We partner directly with farms across Hawaii, Ethiopia, and Colombia — paying farmers fairly and bringing you coffee that tells a story in every cup.

Every batch is small. Every roast is intentional. No shortcuts.
