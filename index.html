Our Story

Rooted in the Earth,
Raised on Good Coffee

Loam is the rich, living soil that gives life to what grows. It's what grounds us. We partner directly with farms across Hawaii, Ethiopia, and Colombia — paying farmers fairly and sourcing coffee that carries the character of the land it came from.

Every batch is small. Every roast is deliberate. No shortcuts, no noise.
