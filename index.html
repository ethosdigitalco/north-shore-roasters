Our Story

Rooted in the earth,
raised on good coffee.

Loam is the rich, living soil that gives life to what grows — it's what grounds us. We partner directly with farms in Hawaii, Ethiopia, and Colombia, paying farmers fairly and sourcing beans that carry the character of the land they came from.

Every batch is small. Every roast is deliberate. No shortcuts, no noise.
