About Us

Coffee is more
than a drink.

It is a moment to slow down, connect,
and come back to yourself.

What started as a small idea between two friends quickly grew into something more meaningful. After years of traveling, working, and chasing fast-paced lives, we found ourselves craving something simple. A space where people could gather, breathe, and enjoy really good coffee without the noise.

So we built it.

Our Story

Founded with a
simple goal.

Loam Roastry was founded in 2021 with a simple goal: create a space that feels grounded, intentional, and human.

We spent months sourcing beans from small, ethical farms across Central and South America, working directly with growers who care deeply about their craft. Every roast we serve is selected not just for its flavor, but for the story behind it.

"The name 'Loam' comes from the idea that life moves us in unexpected ways. Like driftwood, we all land somewhere eventually. We wanted to create a place worth landing."

The Space

Built to feel
calm and welcoming.

Designed with natural materials, soft light, and an open layout. You won't find loud distractions here. Just good music, warm tones, and the steady rhythm of coffee being made.

Why We Do It

Because the
world moves fast.

And we think there's value in creating places where it doesn't have to.
